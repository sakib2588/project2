Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Effective: April 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

Nexora Digital LLC ("Nexora Digital," "we," "us," or "our") is a digital marketing agency based in the United States. We provide website design, SEO, social media marketing, e-commerce solutions, paid advertising, and business profile management services.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@nexoradigital.com or call (310) 955-1077.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, phone number, service interest, and message
  • Newsletter sign-ups — email address
  • Phone and email inquiries — any information you share when contacting us directly

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, such as your browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited, through analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
  • Send you marketing communications if you have opted in (newsletter subscribers)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. We use server-side API keys only — your information is never exposed to the public internet through our front-end code.

We retain contact form submissions and subscriber data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. These tools collect anonymized data including pages viewed, time on site, and traffic sources.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that some features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — database storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Vercel — website hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Google Analytics — website analytics (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Fonts — font delivery

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing its data practices.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority (EU/UK residents)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@nexoradigital.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
